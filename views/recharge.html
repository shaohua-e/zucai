Deposit Funds

  • Paybill Number: 955100 or 955700

    1 Go to Mpesa menu

    2 Select Payment services

    3 Click on Paybill

    4 Enter business number as 955100 or 955700

    5 Enter the account number as SPORTPESA

    6 Enter the amount you want to transfer to SportPesa account

    7 Enter your Mpesa pin and Confirm the request

    8 You will shortly receive an SMS from Mpesa and SportPesa to confirm the transaction

  • Paybill Number: SPORTPESA

    1 Go to Airtel Money Menu

    2 Go to make payment

    3 Select Paybill

    4 Click on Others

    5 Enter business number as SPORTPESA

    6 Enter amount you want to transfer to SportPesa account

    7 Enter your Airtel Money PIN

    8 Enter the reference number as FOOTBALL The request will be sent automatically. You will shortly receive an SMS from Airtel Money and SportPesa to confirm the transaction
